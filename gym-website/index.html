About The Gym

Built By Lifters, For Lifters.

IRONCLAD opened its doors as a no-nonsense training floor for people who wanted more than a treadmill and a mirror. Today we're a full training studio: free weights, conditioning, small-group coaching and recovery, run by coaches who actually program your progress instead of just checking you in.

Our Mission

To make serious, structured training accessible to every person who walks through our doors — regardless of where they're starting from.
